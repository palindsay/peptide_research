## Content Architecture

The main guides are organized into compound categories: GLP-1 agonists, healing/repair peptides, growth hormone secretagogues, anti-aging, nootropics, skin/hair, immune-modulating, reproductive, Russian bioregulators (Khavinson peptides), cardiovascular, and blends. Each guide also includes a vendor directory, safety ratings glossary, and primary source references (PubMed/NCBI, FDA.gov, ClinicalTrials.gov).

Vendor ratings use a standardized rubric across purity (HPLC/MS testing results), certificate of analysis availability, price competitiveness, customer service, and shipping reliability. The A–F rating scale is shared between the Finnrick and PeptideCritic guides.

## Editing Guidelines

- **Cross-document consistency**: Safety ratings (A = research-grade safe → F = avoid) and vendor scores must be synchronized when updating any single document.
- **Source citations**: Link to primary sources only — PubMed (PMID numbers), FDA.gov, or ClinicalTrials.gov (NCT numbers). Avoid secondary/aggregator sources.
- **VERIFIED designation**: Files marked "VERIFIED" have had entries cross-checked against primary sources. Do not apply this label to unverified additions.
- **Version strategy**: Treat `v4_0` as the canonical current version. Earlier versions are retained for diffing and historical reference, not for active editing.
