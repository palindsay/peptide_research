## Rating Methodology Overview

Finnrick Analytics is an independent peptide testing organization that procures samples from vendors and sends them to commercial HPLC laboratories for identity, purity, and potency analysis. Each sample receives a score from 0 to 10 based on three components: Purity (0-4 pts), Quantity Accuracy (0-4 pts), and Batch Information (0-2 pts).

### Finnrick Rating Scale

| Rating | Label | Avg Score | Min Score | Min Tests |
|--------|-------|-----------|-----------|----------|
| A | Great | 7+ | 6+ | 2 |
| B | Good | 6+ | 5+ | 2 |
| C | Okay | 5+ | 4+ | 2 |
| D | Poor | 4+ | 3+ | 2 |
| E | Bad | 0+ | 0+ | 2 |
| F | Fraud | n/a | n/a | n/a |
| X | Unrated | n/a | n/a | <2 |

Vendors with a rating range (e.g., A to C) have different ratings across different products.
