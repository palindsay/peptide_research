## 📊 Ranking Methodology
Because vendor testing and review ecosystems are fragmented, this directory utilizes a **Consensus-Driven Ranking** strategy. Vendors are grouped into tiers based on cross-source validation:
*   **Tier 1 (The Consensus Elite):** Vendors that hold strong ratings across *multiple* independent tracking sources.
*   **Tier 2 (Top Single-Source Performers):** Vendors with exceptional performance on a single major list, but lacking data on others.
*   **Tier 3 (Good/Mixed Verified):** Vendors with moderate or mixed reviews but possessing verifiable third-party testing.
*   **Tier 4 (Unverified / Emerging):** Vendors with perfect ratings but very low review counts or sample sizes.
*   **The Warning List:** Vendors explicitly flagged for fraud, shill tactics, or operating clone scam sites.

## 💡 Purchasing Recommendations & Safety Guide
1.  **Always Verify COAs:** Do not trust a vendor's word. Verify the Certificate of Analysis directly with the 3rd-party lab (e.g., Janoshik, MZ Biolabs) using the batch number.
2.  **Beware of Impersonators:** Scammers clone Telegram accounts of popular Chinese vendors. *Vendors will never DM you first.*
3.  **Payment Security:** Most overseas vendors require Cryptocurrency (USDT-TRON is cheapest/fastest). Be hyper-vigilant when sending crypto.
4.  **Record Unboxings:** Many overseas vendors require a continuous, uncut unboxing video to process refunds for broken or missing vials.
