## 💡 Community Tips from GLP-1 Forum Veterans

- **Diversify vendors** on first purchases — don't put all your eggs in one basket
- **Always verify** rep identity through official channels; vendors will NEVER DM you first
- **Beware of scammers** impersonating reps on Telegram and Discord
- **Request COAs** (Certificates of Analysis) before purchasing — Janoshik is the gold-standard lab
- **Consider independent testing** (PeptideTest USA, Janoshik) for your first orders
- **Record yourself opening packages** on video for potential claims
- **Use email** as the most secure communication channel (less susceptible to impersonation)
- **Crypto payments** (especially USDT-TRON) are fastest and cheapest; PayPal carries handling fees
- **Vendor reputations change fast** — always check the latest forum posts before ordering
- **MOQ (Minimum Order Quantity)** is typically $300 for free shipping from most vendors
- **US Warehouses** offer 2–7 day delivery; China warehouses take 7–18 days
- **Janoshik** (janoshik.com) is the community-standard third-party testing lab
